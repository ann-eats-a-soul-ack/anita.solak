Anita Solak is a poet, writer and editor living and working on unceded Wurundjeri land. She plays with electronic-literature and the failures of language. Her digi-lit, essays and poetry can be found in Australian Multilingual Writing Project, Cordite, Going Down Swinging, Runway Journal, and The Suburban Review among others. When performing poetry she experiments with live interactive literature, creating space for collaborative performances between communities and artists.
